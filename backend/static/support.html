Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start sharing my location?

To share your location:

  1. Log in with your Discord account
  2. Enable location sharing for specific Discord servers in Settings
  3. Grant location permission when prompted

Who can see my location?

Only members of Discord servers where you've explicitly enabled location sharing can see your location. You can also block specific users from seeing your location in Settings.

How accurate is the location sharing?

For privacy reasons, locations are anonymized to within a 2km radius by default. Your precise location is never sent to our servers. You can adjust this radius in Settings.

How do I stop sharing my location?

You can stop location sharing at any time by:

  • Disabling sharing for specific servers in Settings
  • Logging out of the app
  • Revoking location permission for the app in your device settings
